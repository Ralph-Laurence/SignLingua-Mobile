Let's complete your registration

You're just a few steps away from becoming an ASL tutor! Let's verify your identity by submitting a valid ID.

To verify your SignLingua account, you'll need one valid, unexpired, and undamaged government-issued ID from the list below. After submitting, your application will be reviewed, and you'll be notified of its status, typically within 24 hours.

Supported ID Types:
  1. PhilSys National ID
    Card or Paper Type, Digital National ID
  2. Passport
    Travel document, Primary national identity
  3. Driver's License
    Official LTO-issued identification card
  4. UMID (Unified Multi-Purpose Identification)
    SSS, GSIS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG
  5. PRC ID
    Official Proof of Licensure / General Identification
Please ensure your ID meets the following requirements:
  • Take clear, well-lit photos of both the front and back sides of your ID.
  • The ID's details must match the information you provided during registration.
  • No masks or sunglasses are worn.
  • Must be valid, unexpired, and undamaged government-issued ID

You may choose to cancel the registration or proceed to take a photo of your ID. Full access to features and services will remain limited until verification is complete.
